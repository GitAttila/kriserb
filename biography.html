kristýna erbenová

(1987, Czech Republic)

I‘m mainly interested in art photography with a focus on people, landscapes and the relationship between them. In my photography, I‘m trying to bring together the inner world of feelings and thoughts with the outside world of appearances; to create an imaginary space of timelessness; to capture the moments which lead us to a deeper experience of ourselves and the space around us.

All my personal projects are shot on medium format film.

I'm interested in commissions, collaborations, exhibitions and residences.
